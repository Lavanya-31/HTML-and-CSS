Are you curious to know what is competitive exam? You have come to the right place as I am going to tell you everything about competitive exam in a very simple explanation. Without further discussion let’s begin to know what is competitive exam? In today’s competitive world, acquiring the right skills and knowledge is essential for career advancement and personal growth. One common means of evaluating and selecting candidates for educational programs and job opportunities is through competitive exams. In this blog, we’ll explore what competitive exams are, their significance, and how individuals can prepare for success on these challenging assessments. Competitive exams are standardized assessments designed to identify and select individuals based on their performance and aptitude. These exams are characterized by a high level of competition, with numerous candidates vying for a limited number of positions or opportunities. Competitive exams can cover a wide range of subjects and are used for various purposes, including admission to educational institutions, government job recruitment, and professional certification.

UPSC


The Civil Services Examination (CSE) is a national competitive examination in India conducted by the Union Public Service Commission for recruitment to higher Civil Services of the Government of India, including the Indian Administrative Service, Indian Foreign Service, and Indian Police Service.

KPSC


The Civil Services Examination is based on the British era Imperial Civil Service tests, as well as the civil service tests conducted by old Indian empires such as the Mauryan Empire and Mughal Empire. It is one of the most difficult competitive examinations in India.

SSC


Tests the candidate's skills in comprehension, interpersonal skills, communication, logical reasoning, analytical ability, decision-making, problem-solving, basic numeracy, data interpretation, English language comprehension skills, and mental ability.[16] It is qualifying, and the marks obtained in this paper are not counted.
